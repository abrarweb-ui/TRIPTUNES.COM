Service 02

Houseboat Bookings on Dal Lake

A night on a Dal Lake houseboat is one of Kashmir's most unique experiences. We work with established, well-maintained houseboats in prime locations — from the quiet corners of Nagin Lake to the bustling Main Boulevard area.

All our houseboat recommendations are inspected for quality of accommodation, meals, and hospitality. We book only the ones we would personally stay in.

  • Dal Lake and Nagin Lake locations
  • Standard, Deluxe, and Premium categories
  • Shikara ride included on most packages
  • Meals arranged on request
  • Suitable for families, couples, and solo travellers

We design honeymoon packages that focus on privacy, comfort, and special moments. From a private Shikara ride at sunset to a cosy houseboat with mountain views — every detail is arranged thoughtfully.

  • Private Shikara rides on Dal Lake
  • Specially selected romantic stays
  • Mughal Gardens and Tulip Garden visits
  • Customisable based on your preferences
  • Both budget and premium options
