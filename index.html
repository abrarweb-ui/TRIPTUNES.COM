Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you arrange complete Kashmir tour packages? +
Yes. We arrange complete Kashmir tour packages for families, couples, groups, and solo travellers — covering hotel stays, transport, sightseeing, and houseboat bookings. We also build custom itineraries based on your dates and budget.
How do I book a houseboat on Dal Lake? +
Call or WhatsApp us with your travel dates and the number of people. We will suggest the best houseboat options in your budget range and make the booking on your behalf. No advance payment is required in most cases.
Are your cab drivers trustworthy and experienced? +
Yes. All drivers we work with are locally based, hold valid commercial driving licences, and have several years of experience navigating Kashmir's roads — including mountain routes to Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonamarg.
What is the best time to visit Kashmir? +
Kashmir is beautiful year-round. April to June is ideal for flowers, green meadows, and mild weather. December to February offers snowfall and skiing in Gulmarg. September and October bring golden autumn colours in Pahalgam.
Can you arrange airport pickup from Srinagar Airport? +
Yes, we provide airport pickup and drop services at Srinagar Airport at any time of day. Share your flight details and we will have a driver ready when you land.
