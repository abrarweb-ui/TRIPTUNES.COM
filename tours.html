Package Inclusions & Notes

Our packages are quoted on a per-person basis and include accommodation, transport, and listed activities.

Usually Included

  • Accommodation (budget/deluxe/premium based on package)
  • Private cab transport throughout the trip
  • Sightseeing at listed destinations
  • Airport pickup and drop
  • Support from our team throughout

Usually Not Included

  • Gondola tickets (Gulmarg)
  • Personal shopping or meals unless specified

  • Travel insurance
