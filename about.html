Our Story

Started in Budgam, Built on Trust

Trip Tunes Tour and Travels was started by a group of people who grew up in Kashmir and wanted travellers to experience the valley the way locals know it — not through a package brochure, but through genuine connections, honest advice, and careful planning.

Our office is at Safvi Galleria, New Bus Stand Budgam — a real, walkable location where you can come and sit with us before booking anything. We started small, arranging cabs and hotel stays for friends and family. Over time, word spread, and today we serve hundreds of travellers every year from across India.

"We do not just book trips — we make sure the trip you get is the one you imagined."

Every package we offer has been designed based on actual feedback from travellers and the reality of how Kashmir works. We know which routes get congested in peak summer, which houseboats maintain their standards, and which mountain roads need extra planning in winter.

Our Expertise

We Know Kashmir Personally, Not Just Professionally

Our team members are from Srinagar and the surrounding areas of Budgam. We have grown up in this valley — our families are here, our networks are here. That means when we recommend a hotel, it is because we have actually visited it. When we say a route is scenic, it is because we have driven it ourselves.

This local presence gives our clients something that large online travel portals simply cannot offer — real, up-to-date information about conditions on the ground, and genuine care about the experience you will have.
