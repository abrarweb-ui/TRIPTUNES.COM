Trip Tunes Tour and Travels ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

When you make an inquiry, fill in a form, or contact us through any channel on our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Travel dates and trip preferences
  • Any other details you voluntarily share in your message

We do not collect payment card information or financial data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry or booking request
  • To plan and arrange your Kashmir trip as requested
  • To communicate with you about your booking
  • To send relevant updates about your trip (if you are a confirmed client)

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With hotels, houseboats, or transport providers — only to the extent necessary to complete your booking
  • When required by law or a legal authority

We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Inquiry data submitted through our website is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore. We retain inquiry data for a reasonable period to manage follow-ups and improve our service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to receive communications from us

6. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies, primarily for analytics and performance. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core website functionality.

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
